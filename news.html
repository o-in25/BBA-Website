Billow Beach News
Keep up to date with all the latest news from Billow Beach

Audits

The BBA financial records for the 2014-2015 fiscal year will be audited in July 2015 and the 2015-2016 records will be audited before the June 2016 annual meeting.

Suggested Amendments

The suggested amendments to the By-Laws and Constitution will be made available to all Billow Beach residents prior to the a meeting to vote to approve/disapprove

Financial Records

Two committees have been established to audit the BBA financial records and to review the by-laws and Constitution for amendments. Paul Lapolla, George Remias, and Dean Voye will do the audit, and Susan Joyce and Lew Ayres will do the review.

ACDES

The ACDES has been contacted regarding the sewers in Billow Beach. Mark Verzella, sanitary sewer supervisor, was also on the site on 7/17 to explain our private sewer layout and to answer any questions. He that a high level of water under the manhole cover indicates a blockage in the mainline and would affect multiple cottages. The BBA has assumed full responsibility for this type of blockage. Blockage from the mainline to the a cottage is the owner's responsibility.
